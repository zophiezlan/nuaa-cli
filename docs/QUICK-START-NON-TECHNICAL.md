## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting (When Things Go Wrong)

### Problem: "I clicked the link but nothing happened"

**Solution:**
1. Make sure you're connected to the internet
2. Try copying the link and pasting it into your browser
3. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)

### Problem: "It says Python is not installed"

**Solution (Windows):**
1. Go to: https://www.python.org/downloads/
2. Click the big yellow "Download Python" button
3. Run the installer
4. **IMPORTANT:** Check the box that says "Add Python to PATH"
5. Click "Install Now"
6. Wait for it to finish
7. Try the setup again

**Solution (Mac):**
1. Open Terminal (press Cmd+Space, type "terminal", press Enter)
2. Type: `python3 --version` and press Enter
3. If it says "command not found", you need to install Python:
   - Install Homebrew: https://brew.sh
   - Then type: `brew install python3`

**OR:** Just ask a colleague or your coordinator for help!

### Problem: "I closed the window and now it doesn't work"

**Solution:**
- Run the setup again (double-click the file)
- The window needs to stay open while you use NUAA
- Minimize it instead of closing it

### Problem: "I can't find the file I created"

**Solution:**
- All files are saved in the `outputs` folder
- Ask your coordinator where this is on your computer
- Or look in: `[your-nuaa-folder]/outputs/[your-team]/[today's-date]/`

### Problem: "My team isn't listed"

**Solution:**
- Contact your coordinator - they can add your team
- Or email: tech@nuaa.org.au

## ğŸ¤” Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to know how to code?

**No!** Absolutely not. If you can use Facebook or email, you can use NUAA.

### What's a "terminal" or "command line"?

You don't need to know! The double-click method avoids all that.

### Is my data safe?

Yes! Everything is saved on your device (or your team's server). Nothing goes to the cloud unless you choose to sync it.

### Can I use this on my personal phone?

Yes! Just get the link from your coordinator.

### What if I make a mistake?

Don't worry! You can always fill out another form. Nothing is permanent until you click Submit.

### Does this cost money?

No! NUAA tools are free for all staff and peer workers.

### Can I share my screen with a client?

Yes, but be mindful of privacy. Make sure no sensitive information from other forms is visible.

### What if I'm not tech-savvy at all?

That's exactly who this is for! Follow the "Ask Someone to Set It Up" option above.
