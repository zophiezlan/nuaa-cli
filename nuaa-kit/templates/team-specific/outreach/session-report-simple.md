## How to Submit This Report

### Option 1: Email (Easiest)
1. Take a photo of this completed form
2. Email to: outreach@nuaa.org.au
3. Subject line: "Outreach Report - [Location] - [Date]"

### Option 2: SMS Quick Update
Text the following to [number]:
"OUTREACH [Location] [# people] [# needles] [any urgent notes]"

Example: "OUTREACH KingsX 20 people 45 needles all good"

### Option 3: Web Form
1. Go to: nuaa.org.au/outreach-report
2. Enter the information from this sheet
3. Click Submit

### Option 4: Hand in at Office
Bring completed form to NUAA office
