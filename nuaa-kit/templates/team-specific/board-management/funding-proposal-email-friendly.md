# Funding Proposal Template (Email-Friendly)

**For**: Board members, management, project leads
**Completion Method**: Answer questions via email
**Time Required**: 30-60 minutes (can be completed in stages)
**Technical Skills**: None required - just reply to email prompts

---

## How to Use This Template (Email Method)

**Option 1: Answer All Questions at Once**
1. Read through all questions below
2. Write your answers in a Word document or email
3. Send to: proposals@nuaa.org.au
4. Subject: "Funding Proposal - [Project Name]"
5. We'll generate the professional proposal and send it back to you

**Option 2: Answer Questions One at a Time**
1. Email: proposals@nuaa.org.au with subject "Start New Proposal"
2. We'll email you one question at a time
3. Reply with your answer
4. We'll send the next question
5. Takes about 1 week completing 5-10 minutes per day

**Option 3: Phone Call**
1. Call [number] to arrange a time
2. We'll call you and ask the questions
3. We'll take notes and create the proposal
4. You review and approve the final version

## Next Steps

Once you've answered these questions:

### Submit Your Answers

**Email to**: proposals@nuaa.org.au

**Subject line**: "Funding Proposal - [Your Project Name]"

**What to include**:
1. Your answers to all questions above
2. Any supporting documents
3. Your contact details
4. Deadline for funder application (if applicable)

---

### What Happens Next

1. **We receive your answers** (same day)
2. **We create a draft proposal** (2-3 business days)
3. **We send it to you for review** (via email)
4. **You provide feedback or approval** (via email or phone)
5. **We finalize and send you the proposal** (1 business day)
6. **You submit to funder** (you can do this, or we can help)

---

### Timeline

**Total time from submission to final proposal**: 5-7 business days

**Rush available?**: Yes, for urgent deadlines (let us know)

## Tips for Good Answers

✓ **Be specific**: Numbers, names, locations help
✓ **Tell stories**: Real examples are powerful
✓ **Use plain language**: No jargon needed
✓ **It's okay to be brief**: We can expand your answers
✓ **Don't worry about writing quality**: We'll polish it
✓ **Ask for help**: We're here to support you

---

## Examples

**Bad answer**: "We'll do outreach"

**Good answer**: "We'll conduct weekly outreach in King's Cross every Friday night from 8pm-midnight, providing NSP supplies, harm reduction information, and referrals to approximately 30-50 people who inject drugs"

---

**Bad answer**: "People will be safer"

**Good answer**: "Participants will have increased knowledge of overdose prevention (measured by pre/post surveys), access to Naloxone (80% uptake goal), and confidence in harm reduction practices (measured by feedback forms)"
