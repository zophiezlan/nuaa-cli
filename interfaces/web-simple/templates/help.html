Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new document? +

To create a new document:

  1. Go to your team dashboard
  2. Click on the template you want to use
  3. Fill out the form fields
  4. Click "Submit Document" when done

Your document will be saved automatically in the outputs folder.

Can I save my work and continue later? +

Yes! NUAA Tools has an auto-save feature that saves your work every 30 seconds. You can also manually save a draft by clicking the "Save Draft" button.

When you return to the same form, you'll be asked if you want to load your saved draft.

How do I add photos to my report? +

There are several ways to add photos:

  • Camera: Click the "Take Photo" button to use your device's camera
  • Upload: Drag and drop files into the upload area, or click "Choose Files"
  • Mobile: On phones, you can select photos from your gallery or take new ones
Can I use voice input instead of typing? +

Yes! Click the microphone icon (üé§) next to any text field to use voice input. Simply speak and your words will be converted to text.

Note: Voice input requires a modern browser and microphone access.

Does this work offline? +

Yes, once the page is loaded, it works offline. Your data is saved locally on your device, so you can continue working without an internet connection.

However, some features like sharing to Teams or SharePoint require an internet connection.

Where are my documents saved? +

Documents are saved in the outputs folder, organized by:

  • Team name
  • Date (YYYY-MM-DD)
  • Template name and timestamp

Example: outputs/outreach/2025-11-18/session-report-20251118-143022.md

Can I export documents as PDF or Word? +

Yes! Use the export buttons in the dashboard or preview modal to export your documents as:

  • PDF - For printing or sharing
  • Word (.docx) - For further editing
  • Excel - For data analysis (certain templates)
How do I use accessibility features? +

Click the accessibility icon (‚ôø) in the top navigation to access:

  • High Contrast Mode: Better visibility for low vision users
  • Large Text Mode: Bigger fonts for easier reading
  • Screen Reader Support: Fully compatible with screen readers
  • Keyboard Navigation: Use Tab, Enter, and arrow keys to navigate
