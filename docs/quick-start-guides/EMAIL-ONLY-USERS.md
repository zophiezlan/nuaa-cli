**For**: Anyone who uses email (no other computer skills needed!)

**Time to read**: 2 minutes

---

---

## How It Works (3 Easy Steps)

### Step 1: Send an Email

**To**: `nuaa@nuaa.org.au`

**Subject**: What you want to create

Examples:

- "Outreach Report"
- "Distribution Log"
- "Help Me Choose" (if you're not sure)

**Body**: You can leave this empty, or write what you know

---

### Step 2: Answer the Questions

You'll get an email back with a question.

**Just reply with your answer.** That's it!

Example:

```
From: NUAA Tools
Subject: Question 1 of 5

What date was your session?

---
Just reply to this email with your answer.
```

**You reply**: "November 18, 2025"

---

### Step 3: Get Your Document

After you answer all the questions, you'll get your finished document as an email attachment (PDF and Word).

**Done!** Your report is complete and saved.

---

## Real Example

### Example: Peer Distribution Log

**You send**:

```
To: nuaa@nuaa.org.au
Subject: Distribution Log
```

**System replies**:

```
Hi! I'll help you create your distribution log.

Question 1: What date?
```

**You reply**: "Today"

**System replies**:

```
Question 2: How many people did you distribute to?
```

**You reply**: "5 people"

**System replies**:

```
Question 3: What did you distribute?
(Example: 15 needles, 20 swabs)
```

**You reply**: "15 needles, 20 swabs, 10 tourniquets"

**System replies**:

```
Question 4: Do you need resupply?
```

**You reply**: "No"

**System replies**:

```
‚úÖ All done! Your distribution log is attached.

It's also been saved and your coordinator has been notified.

```

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to install anything?**
A: No! Just use your normal email.

**Q: What email should I use?**
A: Any email address works (Gmail, Hotmail, work email, etc.)

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**
A: No problem! Reply "back" or ask for changes at the end.

**Q: How long does it take?**
A: Usually 5-10 emails total. A few minutes.

**Q: What if I can't finish all at once?**
A: Reply "save" and we'll send you a reminder email to continue.

**Q: Can someone help me?**
A: Yes! Any NUAA staff member can help. Or call [number].
