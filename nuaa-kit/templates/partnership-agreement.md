## 12. Cultural Safety & Ethical Practice

**Harm Reduction Principles**:

- Meeting people where they're at
- Non-judgmental, non-coercive approaches
- Evidence-based information and support
- Respect for autonomy and choice

**Cultural Safety**:

- Respect for Aboriginal & Torres Strait Islander peoples and cultures
- LGBTIQ+ inclusive practices
- Culturally appropriate services for CALD communities
- Accessibility for people with disabilities

**Ethical Practice**:

- Informed consent for all activities and data collection
- Confidentiality and privacy
- Do no harm
- Consumer participation and remuneration ($300/session standard)

## 13. Dispute Resolution

**In the event of disagreement or concern**:

**Step 1: Primary Contacts**
- Issue raised between primary contacts
- Attempt to resolve through discussion
- Timeframe: Within 5 working days

**Step 2: Senior Management**
- If unresolved, escalate to senior management from both orgs
- Meeting to discuss and seek resolution
- Timeframe: Within 10 working days

**Step 3: Formal Mediation**
- If still unresolved, engage independent mediator
- Both parties agree to participate in good faith
- Costs shared equally

**Step 4: Termination** (Last Resort)
- See Section 14 for termination process
